Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical car painting job take?

A standard car painting job usually takes 3-5 business days, depending on the condition of the vehicle, the type of paint being used, and any additional bodywork needed. For custom paint jobs or complete restorations, the timeline may extend to 1-2 weeks.

Do you offer a warranty on your repair work?

Yes, all of our repair and painting work comes with a 2-year warranty covering workmanship and materials. This ensures your peace of mind and demonstrates our confidence in the quality of our services.

Can you match the original color of my car?

Absolutely! We use advanced color-matching technology to ensure that any new paint perfectly matches your car's original color, even accounting for sun fading and aging. Our technicians are skilled in blending techniques that make repairs seamless.

Do you work with insurance companies?

Yes, we regularly work with all major insurance companies in Finland. We can help guide you through the claims process and work directly with your insurance provider to make the repair process as smooth as possible for you.
